    Atmospheric Circulation
    The article comprehensively discusses the concept of atmospheric circulation, its characteristics in different geographical regions, and the impact of seasonal changes. It details the basic mechanisms of atmospheric circulation, such as the uneven distribution of energy, the impact of Earth's rotation on wind direction, and how atmospheric circulation occurs in the troposphere. Additionally, it discusses the four main pressure belts, three wind belts, and how they form a closed circulation system. The article concludes by mentioning the potential impacts of global climate change on atmospheric circulation, emphasizing the impact of polar region heating on the global climate system.
